Port Royal Club — How Sponsorship Works
Port Royal Club has no application process and no waiting list. You cannot pay to join regardless of net worth. Membership requires an existing member to personally sponsor you — meaning they vouch for you socially and put their own standing behind the introduction. The social barrier is higher than the financial one. Path to sponsorship: Build genuine relationships in the Naples ecosystem first — Naples Winter Wine Festival, Baker Museum gala circuit, and Collier County CEO Forum. The sponsorship comes from those relationships, not from asking for it.
