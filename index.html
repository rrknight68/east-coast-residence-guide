Model Overview

Concept
Anchor Auto Rentals acquires durable, depreciated Toyota vehicles (Corolla/Camry) in the $5,000–$7,000 range and rents them on a week-to-week basis to gig economy drivers. Each vehicle is managed as a cash-flow asset with formal contracts, commercial insurance, GPS tracking, deposit requirements, and strict driver screening.
Buy Box
Toyota Corolla 2009–2014 · Toyota Camry 2008–2014 · Price: $5,000–$7,000 · Mileage: 100K–160K max · Clean title required · Cold AC required · Pre-purchase inspection mandatory · No salvage/rebuilt titles
Hold Period & Exit
18–24 months per vehicle. Estimated resale $2,000–$3,000. Proceeds reinvested into newer fleet vehicle.
Phase 2 Entity Structure (3+ Vehicles)
Anchor Assets LLC (owns vehicles) → leases to Anchor Operations LLC (renter-facing, collects payments) at $200/month/vehicle. Separate bank accounts, bookkeeping, and tax filings. Real intercompany lease required.
